Route Updates

Here You Will Find Information On UNC Charlottes Routes

Gold

The Gold route navigates on weekdays through the South Campus Loop going from South Village to Central and North Campus
  • Monday through Friday: 6 a.m. to 2 a.m.
  • Does not run on Weekends
  • Runs the South Campus Loop

Green

The Green Route navigates the North Campus Loop which iterates through North Deck to Central campus and South Village
  • Monday through Friday, 6 a.m. to 2 a.m.
  • Saturday and Sunday, 9 a.m. to 2 a.m.
  • Runs the North Campus Loop

Silver

The Silver Route takes the CRI cross campus route which goes from Lot 6 at John Kirk and Cameron, to East Decks, Student Union and CRI
  • Monday through Friday, 6 a.m. to 2 a.m.
  • Saturday and Sunday, 9 a.m. to 2 a.m.
  • Runs the CRI Cross Campus

For more information please visit the UNCC Campus or website!
